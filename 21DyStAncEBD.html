21DyStAncEBD Series




21st Dynasty Studies on Ancient Egyptian Book of the Dead

ISSN XXXX-XXXX

Series editors: Rita Lucarelli & Ivan Rodríguez López

21DyStAncEBD aims to address the lack of comprehensive studies and editions of numerous papyri from the Third Intermediate Period, particularly the 21st Dynasty. The editors intend to provide a synoptic and rigorous study of selected Book of the Dead spells from 21st Dynasty sources to deepen understanding of the variability within funerary compositions and to shed light on key evidence regarding their transmission and editorial practices from the end of the New Kingdom to the early Third Intermediate Period.
