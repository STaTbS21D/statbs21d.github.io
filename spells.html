Spell Selection

So far, the spell selection criteria are based on the mingled fragments detected in papyrus Havana and the sequences BD 23-28 on many papyri of the period, as well as BD 6 and 193. The spells are clustered according to their theme and the inner logic of the amalgamation, which always match —as a whole, or seen as the internal argument of the fragmentary source in relation to the continent spell.
